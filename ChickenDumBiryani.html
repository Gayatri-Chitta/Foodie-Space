About Chicken Dum Biryani

Chicken Dum Biryani is a classic Indian dish that originates from the Mughlai cuisine. It’s a flavorful, aromatic, and rich one-pot meal where marinated chicken is cooked with fragrant basmati rice, herbs, and spices, all sealed together in a ‘dum’ (slow cooking) process to let the flavors meld beautifully.

How to make Chicken Dum Biryani:

  • Ingredients
    1. 750 gms chicken pieces (clean and wash well)
    2. salt to taste
    3. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    4. ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
    5. 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
    6. 2 small cinnamon sticks (dalchini)
    7. 3 cloves
    8. ½ teaspoon cardamom powder/ elaichi powder
    9. ½ teaspoon shahi zeera
    10. ½ teaspoon garam masala powder
    11. ¾ cup yogurt
    12. 1 teaspoon green chili paste (green chili and coriander leaves blended paste with a pinch of salt)
    13. ½ cup fried onions (crush and add)
    14. ½ teaspoon black pepper powder
    15. 1 tablespoon oil
    16. 3-4 chopped mint leaves
    17. 1 teaspoon lemon extracted juice
    18. 2 tablespoon coriander leaves
    19. Basmati Rice 750 gms
  • Step To Make:

      • Chicken marination:

        • Add salt to the chicken, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ginger garlic paste, cinnamon sticks, cloves.
        • Also, add cardamom powder, shahi zeera, garam masala powder, yogurt, green chili paste, mix well
        • Add crushed fried onions and mix everything well.
        • Add black pepper powder, oil, chopped mint leaves, lemon extracted juice, coriander leaves, mix well.
        • Cover and Marinate in refrigerator for at least 1 to 2 hours.


      • The first step is to take cut chicken pieces, wash them well and drain excess water out. Then add salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ginger garlic paste, whole spices such as cardamom powder, cinnamon, cloves, shahi jeera, garam masala powder.
      • Add Yogurt and green chilli paste. Take some green chillies, few cilantro leaves, add a pinch of salt and make a fine paste. Add green chili paste as per spice requirement.
      • Mix everything well.
      • Add crushed fried onions
      • Add black peppercorn powder, mint leaves, coriander leaves, oil, lemon extract.
      • Mix everything and marinate well for at least 1 to 2 hours in refrigerator.
      • Bring the water to a boil by adding whole spices such as cinnamon stick, cloves, shahi jeera, cardamoms, black peppercorns, salt to taste, oil.
      • Boil well and add soaked basmati rice rice to the water. Soaking basmati rice for atleast 40 to 45 minutes gives long grain rice.
      • Cook the rice until 75% done and strain the rice.
      • Place a Tawa. Place a cooking pot on the Tawa. Placing a griddle below the cooking pot avoids burning the biryani at the base. Add little oil at the base, add the marinated chicken, spread evenly.
      • Add the strained rice. Spread evenly. Do not make layers. Add all the rice
      • Top it with crushed fried onions, chopped coriander and mint leaves, saffron strands soaked in milk,saffron food color, clarified butter/ ghee.
      • Cover tightly with aluminium foil. Cover with a lid
      • Dum on high flame for 10 minutes and on low flame for 30 minutes.
      • Biryani is ready to serve.
