About Brownie Nutella Thickshake

The Brownie Nutella Thickshake is a rich and indulgent dessert drink that combines the best of two beloved treats: brownies and Nutella. This thickshake is a perfect blend of creamy, chocolatey, and nutty flavors, making it a favorite among dessert lovers.

How to make Gooey chocolate cherry cookies

  • Ingredients
    1. 2 brownies
    2. 3 tablespoons Nutella
    3. 2 cups vanilla ice cream
    4. 1 cup milk (adjust for desired thickness).
    5. Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
    6. Chocolate syrup (optional, for garnish)
    7. Crushed nuts or chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)
  • Step To Make:
    1. Prepare the Ingredients:
      • If your brownies are large, break them into smaller pieces for easier blending.
      • Have the Nutella, ice cream, and milk ready.
    2. Blend the Ingredients:
      • In a blender, add the brownie pieces, Nutella, vanilla ice cream, and milk.
      • Blend on high until everything is smooth and creamy. If the shake is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again.
    3. Serve:
      • Pour the thickshake into glasses.
      • Optional: Top with whipped cream, drizzle chocolate syrup, and sprinkle crushed nuts or chocolate shavings for extra flavor and presentation.
    4. Enjoy:
      • Serve immediately while it's cold and creamy!
