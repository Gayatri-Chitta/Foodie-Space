About Gooey chocolate cherry cookies

Gooey Chocolate Cherry Cookies are a delicious treat that combines the rich flavors of chocolate with the sweet tartness of cherries.These cookies are typically soft and chewy,

with a gooey center that oozes chocolate and cherry goodness with every bite.The recipe often includes a combination of dark or milk chocolate, dried or fresh cherries, and sometimes a touch of almond extract to enhance the cherry flavor.

How to make Gooey chocolate cherry cookies

  • Ingredients
    1. 200g unsalted butter, at room temperature
    2. 85g light muscovado sugar
    3. 85g golden caster sugar.
    4. 1 egg.
    5. 225g self-raising flour.
    6. 50g plain chocolate, 50-70% cocoa, roughly chopped.
    7. 50g white chocolate, roughly chopped.
    8. 85g natural colour glacé cherry, roughly chopped.
  • Step 1
    1. Heat oven to 190C/fan 170C/gas 5..
    2. Beat the butter, sugars and egg until smooth, then mix in the flour, chocolates and cherry pieces and ½ tsp salt.
    3. Spoon onto non-stick baking sheets in large rough blobs – you’ll get 20 out of this mix.
    4. Make sure they are well spaced as the cookies grow substantially as they bake.
  • Step 2
    1. Bake for 12-14 mins until just golden, but still quite pale and soft in the middle.
    2. If baking from frozen, give them a few mins more.
    3. Cool on the sheets for 5 mins, then lift onto racks with a fish slice and leave to cool completely.
